--- layout: post title: Is 2009 this crazy for anyone else? published: true tags: [] date: '2009-03-01 15:22:01' posterous_id: 10619160 posterous_url: http://blog.kirkryyn.com/is-2009-this-crazy-for-anyone-else posterous_slug: is-2009-this-crazy-for-anyone-else permalink: is-2009-this-crazy-for-anyone-else/index.html --- I've found myself completely drowning in fires this year. So much has changed. Moving, relaunching and incorporating the gallery. s3a.  Leaving Halogen Creative and forming Dragonfly Development. Finding out I have been selected for an audit. Things are rocky, but least my income is secure, right? Early last week I finally got around to subscribing to Cisco Google News alerts in the hopes that I could stay better in the loop on what is going on with Cisco as the economy continues to be iffy. Barely two days after I set it up, news reports about Cisco cutting as many as 1,500 to 2,500 employees started to trickle in. Such wonderful timing. I get the feeling that late 2008 and early 2009 have actually been pretty crazy for many people. Everything is in flux. This is probably a good thing, but wow. It sure can be stressful...
