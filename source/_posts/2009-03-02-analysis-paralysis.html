--- layout: post title: Analysis paralysis published: true tags: [] date: '2009-03-02 18:09:30' posterous_id: 10619161 posterous_url: http://blog.kirkryyn.com/analysis-paralysis posterous_slug: analysis-paralysis-3 permalink: analysis-paralysis-3/index.html --- I love it when I come to realize that software patterns and anti-patterns apply to things outside of software. Some more so than others. Not Invented Here is an anti-pattern that has quite clearly had an impact on my life. Analysis paralysis is another anti-pattern that I often find entering my life more often than I would really like. Several projects are currently suffering from the effects of prolonged analysis paralysis. I started to realize this yesterday, and I believe that is usually the first step towards addressing the problem and moving forward. Baby steps.
