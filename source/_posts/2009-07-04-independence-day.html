My wife and I watched the 4th of July fireworks together tonight. My wife. It is going to take some time getting used to that but it is already starting to feel pretty natural!

So much has happened this year! Let me see.... engaged, married, almost every member of my family has now been to Seattle, my family has grown considerably, friends had their baby, I started two businesses, survived an audit, I went camping (in my own tent!), started several open source projects (now to finish them!), started a few closed source projects, learned a new programming language... I know I'm missing a few big things and tons of little ones, but wow! Just these things? And it is only July? Wow.

I've given up on the idea that things are going to settle down quickly. Time to just run with it.
